What are In-Betweens?

In-Between shape keys are intermediate shapes that modify other shape keys. They allow more precise control over deformation and are particularly useful for facial animation.

In-betweens are offered natively in many 3D software packages and are considered indispensible by professional riggers and animators. In-Betweens brings a flexible implementation to Blender that is easy-to-use while offering advanced features that not only match but exceed the capabilities of other software packages.
